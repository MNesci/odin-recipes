Kimchi

Description

This kimchi recipe has been passed down my family for generations. It is simple, but elegant and flavourful. Just about anyone can make this kimchi with just a little bit of practice, and that practice will pay off when it comes time to eat!

Ingredients

  • 1 medium head of napa cabbage (about 2 pounds)
  • 1/4 of cup sea salt
  • water
  • 1 tbs of grated garlic
  • 1 tsp of granulated sugar
  • 2 tbs of fish sauce
  • 1 tbs of red pepper flakes
  • 8 ounces of peeled and cut matchsticks
  • 4 medium scallions

Steps

  1. Cut the cabbage lengthwise into quarters and then into 2-inch-wide strips
  2. Sprinkle and massage salt into the cabbage
  3. Cover the cabbage with water, place a plate on it, weight the plate down, and let sit for 1-2 hours

  6. Stir pepper flakes into the paste
  7. Combine cabbage with the paste
  8. Mix thoroughly
  9. Pack into a jar, pressing down until the brine covers the cabbage
  10. Let ferment to 1-5 days, refridgerating once sufficiently ripened
