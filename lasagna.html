Lasagna

Description

This lasagna recipe has been passed down my family for generations. It is simple, but elegant and flavourful. Just about anyone can make this lasagna with just a little bit of practice, and that practice will pay off when it comes time to eat!

Ingredients

  • 500 grams of lasagna noodles
  • 3 cups of ground beef pasta sauce
  • 1/2 cup of grated mozzarella cheese
  • 2 cups of ricotta cheese

Steps

  1. Add a layer of sauce (about a quarter of your sauce) in a pan
  2. Add a layer of ricotta cheese (about a quarter of your ricotta) in the pan
  3. Add a layer of noodles (about a quarter of your noodles) in the pan
  4. Repeat the above steps 3 more times
  5. Sprinkes the mozzarella on top
  6. Preheat over to 400 degrees Farenheit
  7. Cook for 45 minutes
