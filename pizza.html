Pizza

Description

This pizza recipe has been passed down my family for generations. It is simple, but elegant and flavourful. Just about anyone can make this pizza with just a little bit of practice, and that practice will pay off when it comes time to eat!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of pizza dough
  • 1/2 cups of pizza sauce
  • 1 cup of grated mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup of sliced pepperoni

Steps

  1. Roll out the dough
  2. Spread the sauce on the dough
  3. Sprinkle cheese on the dough
  4. Evenly distribute pepperoni on the dough
  5. Preheat oven to 400 degrees Farenheit
  6. Cook pizza for 15 minutes
